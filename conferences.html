Conferences

Inner Healing and Deliverance Conference

The Inner Healing and Deliverance Conference is a 7-day residential programme that aims at touching and transforming lives, ministering to those who are emotionally, physically and spiritually broken. This ministry of inner healing and deliverance is not an end in itself but a means for effective discipleship. We desire to see people restored and able to disciple nations through these conferences.

Prayer Ministry Seminar

It is our desire to see people restored from brokenness to wholeness and be equipped in the ministry of Healing and Deliverance. The purpose behind this seminar is to impart a message of hope to the hurting by providing the steppingstones to equip people to minister to those in need effectively. On completing the ‘Prayer Ministry Seminar’ you will be equipped with the skills and practical application in which you can grow in discernment.
